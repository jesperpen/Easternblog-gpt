Preserving communist heritage

The Eastern Blog maps the remains of communist and Soviet-era visual culture: mosaics on housing blocks, Lenin statues in small towns, palaces of culture in fading factory cities and forgotten bus stops along rural roads.

This is not nostalgia for a political system but a visual record of an era that still shapes streetscapes across Europe, Asia and beyond.
