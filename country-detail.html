Moldova

Moldova is a small landlocked country between Romania and Ukraine with a rich mix of Romanian, Soviet and local visual culture. Soviet mosaics and monuments can still be found in housing districts, factory towns and rural villages.

Key facts

Capital: Chișinău

Population: 2.6 million

Language: Romanian (Moldovan)

Currency: Moldovan leu (MDL)

Independence: 1991

Local flavour

Popular beer: Chișinău

National dish: Mămăligă

Where Soviet traces show

Housing districts, former factory towns, bus stations and Soviet spa resorts.
